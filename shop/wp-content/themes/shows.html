ProScore are the leaders in sports & charity fundraising events.

We have a unique combination of sport and music—a mix that is definitely a winner. Meet some of the greats of Rugby League and all other sports, combined with great music from the 80’s, 90’s to now.

Eric Grothe Snr (Ex Parramatta Eels/State of Origin and Australian Player), together with his band “The Gurus” are great musicians who will entertain your guests throughout the evening. Combined with the band we have great guest speakers such as: Paul Sironen, Billy Moore, Peter Tunks, Brett Kenny, Wally Lewis to mention a few.

Guest speaker Bill Harrigan is often a guest singer with the Guru’s

The night was a great success due to the organisation from our club volunteers and Sid’s easy to follow instructions on how to have a great night and make money at the same time.

For our season launch the Narromine Jets decided to invite Sid Barnes and special guest speakers Brett Kenny and Eric Grothe Snr. The night was a great success due to the organisation from our club volunteers and Sid’s easy to follow instructions on how to have a great night and make money at the same time.

Brett and Eric spoke openly and passionately of their life and times in rugby league as well as answering an avalanche of questions from the crowd, Sid emceed the night and ensured everyone present was involved as well as the fabulous auction of superb quality sporting memorabilia.

After the boys had finished talking about the good times Eric joined his band the ‘Gurus’ and played on through to the end, the band was absolutely terrific and you need to do yourself a favour and go listen to these great entertainers.

Brett and Eric mingled with everyone throughout the night and were only too happy to pose for photos and sign autographs. The bonus of the night was the money raised and the extra commission that was reimbursed to our club through the auction.

The Narromine Jets have no trouble in recommending Sid and his team to anyone looking for a great time and a way to make some money for their club.

Thanks guys

Mick Burns

Narromine Jets Rugby League Club Secretary

THE COMMITTEE: BATHURST ST PATRICKS RLFC

Recently Bathurst St Patrick’s RLFC held a Sportsman’s Dinner/Season Launch using the services of Sid Barnes and the team at ProScore.

The evening was organised over a number of months with Sid providing tremendous assistance and expertise in how to organise the evening and how to promote it in the community and within our club.

The memorabilia that Sid provided was 1stclass and the way that Sid conducted the auctions ensured that they went for a healthy sum. This of course helped our club earn a good return on the night.

Special mention should be made of the two speakers that Sid arranged in Peter Tunks and Brett Kenny. Both Peter & Brett were absolute gentleman making time to talk to everyone, posing for photos and being outstanding representatives of the game. Their talks where funny, very on topic and interesting for all. In speaking to attendee’s everyone agreed it was a great night.

In short our club would not hesitate to recommend to any other CRL Club to use the services of Sid and his team at ProScore. We are already planning our next function with them.

The Committee of Bathurst St Patricks
